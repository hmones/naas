The Network of Arab Alternative Screens (NAAS) is currently seeking a Freelance Web Developer to dedicate 6 hours/month for the maintenance of its trilingual website (English, Arabic, German) and blog (Arabic). Candidates based in the Arabic-speaking region will be given priority.


Required experience and skills:

  • Knowledge of GitHub and Laravel PHP is a must

  • Demonstrated experience with multilingual or bilingual websites

  • Fluency in written and spoken Arabic

  • Familiarity with OctoberCMS and WordPress are also a plus


About the position:

The contract for this position is a freelance retainer contract based on a monthly cap of 6 hours for maintenance work. It begins with a trial period of 6 months, with the possibility of extension.

The fee is an honorarium in Euros based on the workload, which would be paid out after the provision of a tax invoice. 


Interested candidates can send their CV and quotation listing an hourly rate to info@naasnetwork.org
