Since 2016, NAAS has engaged in sector-wide research programs aiming to provide organizational, programming and financial information on its member spaces,  as well as a better understanding of cinema-going experiences of audiences in the region today. In June 2016, NAAS launched Malaffat, a research blog that consists of a platform for critical writing and discussion around cinema as a unique artistic and social institution.
